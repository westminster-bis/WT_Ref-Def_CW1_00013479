"The Impossible Becomes Habitual"

This is the philosophy of the Company. This is the basis on which UNION SPACE was created, and this is the value that the Company has been striving for throughout its history. Today UNION SPACE is the largest retailer representing world famous brands in Uzbekistan.

"UNION SPACE" is more than 300 employees who create the most stylish and inspiring shopping experience.

UNION SPACE begins its history in 1996, when the first company store was opened in Tashkent and it was a NIKE store in TASHKENT PLAZA.
Since then, we have not only won the trust of customers, expanded the geography of our presence, but also formed the main postulates of the corporate culture, which we impeccably follow.

Working as a close-knit team, we strive to become even better, helping customers to be brighter, more beautiful, express their own individuality and style through beautiful clothes at attractive prices.





Today NIKE is one of the world leaders in the production of sportswear and footwear. NIKE is headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon, USA.

The philosophy of Nike remains unchanged: the spirit of sports competition and the spirit of sports nobility, loyalty to tradition and the undying pursuit of excellence. Nike in Uzbekistan is a concept (NSP) store representing the company in this market for over 20 years.
